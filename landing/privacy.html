Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Povver collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal data when you use the Povver mobile application and website (together, the "Service"). Povver is operated by Valterandersson TMI, a sole proprietorship registered in Finland.

By creating an account or using the Service, you consent to the practices described in this policy, including the processing of health-related data as described in Section 3.

1. Data Controller

Povver is the data controller for the personal data processed through the Service. This means we determine the purposes and means of processing your data.

Valterandersson TMI
Finland
Email: privacy@povver.ai

2. What We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name (if provided), and authentication credentials. Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication or Apple Sign-In — we do not store passwords directly.

2.2 Workout and Training Data

The core of the Service involves recording your training. This includes exercises performed, sets, repetitions, weights, RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion), workout dates, durations, personal notes, templates, routines, and training programs. This data may reveal information about your physical capabilities and health status, and we treat it accordingly (see Section 3).

2.3 AI Coaching Data

When you interact with the AI coach, we process the messages you send, the responses generated, and the workout context used to produce those responses. Your workout history and performance trends are provided to the AI model so it can generate personalized coaching. See Section 5 for details on how AI processing works.

2.4 Device and Usage Data

We automatically collect device type, operating system version, app version, feature usage patterns, interaction events, crash reports, and performance diagnostics. We collect country-level location (not precise GPS coordinates).

2.5 Payment Data

All subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details, billing address, or financial information. We receive only a transaction confirmation and subscription status from Apple.

2.6 Website Analytics

Our website (povver.ai) uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized usage data such as page views, referral sources, and device information. This data is collected via cookies and only with your consent. You can withdraw consent or opt out at any time.

2.7 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect biometric identifiers or biometric information (such as fingerprints, facial geometry, voiceprints, or retina scans). We do not collect precise geolocation data. We do not access your device camera, microphone, contacts, or photo library.

3. Health-Related Data and Your Consent

Workout data — such as the weights you lift, your training volume, RPE ratings, and performance trends over time — may reveal information about your physical health, fitness level, and physical capabilities. Some privacy laws classify this type of data as health-related or sensitive personal data, subject to heightened protections.

We take a conservative approach: we treat your workout and training data as sensitive personal data and require your explicit consent to process it. When you create an account and begin logging workouts, you are providing this consent. You may withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account, which will trigger deletion of your personal data (see Section 8).

Specifically, you consent to:

  • Storage of your workout and training data to provide the Service.
  • Processing of your workout data by AI models to generate coaching recommendations and training analysis (see Section 5).
  • Use of anonymized, aggregated workout data to improve the Service. This aggregated data cannot identify you.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Providing the Service — storing your workouts, generating AI coaching recommendations, tracking your training progress, and building personalized programs.
  • AI Coaching — your workout history and messages to the AI coach are sent to our AI model provider (Google Vertex AI) to generate responses. See Section 5 for details.
  • Service Improvement — analyzing anonymized usage patterns, identifying bugs, and developing new features. We do not use your individual workout data for this purpose without anonymization.
  • Communication — sending service-related notifications and responding to support requests. We will only send marketing communications with your explicit opt-in consent.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention — detecting and preventing unauthorized access, abuse, or other harmful activity.
  • Legal Compliance — meeting our obligations under applicable law, including responding to lawful requests from authorities.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your personal data for advertising. We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. How AI Processing Works

Povver uses AI to provide coaching, post-workout analysis, and program generation. The AI coach is an automated system, not a human. When you send messages to the AI coach, those messages are transmitted to a third-party AI infrastructure provider for processing. By using the AI coaching features, you acknowledge and consent to this transmission.

Here is exactly what happens with your data during AI processing:

  • AI Model Provider: We use Google Vertex AI (operated by Google LLC) to run large language models that power the coaching features.
  • What is sent: When you interact with the AI coach or when a post-workout analysis is generated, relevant portions of your workout history, the current conversation context, and your training patterns are transmitted to Google Vertex AI for processing.
  • How it is processed: Google Vertex AI processes this data to generate a response (coaching advice, workout analysis, or a training program). The processing happens on Google's infrastructure. No human reviews your individual conversations.
  • Data retention by the AI provider: Under our agreement with Google, data sent to Vertex AI for inference is not used by Google to train or improve their models. Google's data processing terms apply to this processing.
  • No third-party model training: Your personal workout data is never used to train AI models — not by us, and not by our AI provider.

7. International Data Transfers

Povver is based in Finland (EU). Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area, including the United States, where our third-party service providers maintain infrastructure.

These transfers are protected by:

  • The EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (for transfers to certified U.S. organizations)
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • The UK International Data Transfer Agreement (UK IDTA) for transfers from the United Kingdom

We ensure that any international transfer of your data is accompanied by appropriate safeguards that provide a level of protection equivalent to that available within the EEA.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Account and workout data — retained for as long as your account is active.
  • AI conversation history — retained for the duration of your active use to provide contextual coaching.
  • Analytics data — retained in anonymized, aggregated form. This data cannot identify you.

When you delete your account, we will delete or permanently anonymize all personal data associated with your account within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained indefinitely. Data that we are required by law to retain will be kept for the minimum period required and then deleted.

You can request account deletion at any time through the app or by contacting us at privacy@povver.ai.

9. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to processing of your data based on our legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing that occurred before withdrawal.
  • Opt out of data sharing — we do not sell or share your personal data for advertising purposes. If this changes in the future, we will provide a clear opt-out mechanism.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@povver.ai. We will respond within 30 days. If a request is complex or we receive a large number of requests, we may extend this by an additional 60 days — we will notify you if this is the case.

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. For users in Finland, this is the Office of the Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi). Users in other jurisdictions may contact their local data protection authority.

10. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest
  • Authentication-gated access to all personal data
  • Principle of least privilege for system access
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure and code

No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. While we implement industry-standard protections, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.
  • Notify affected users without undue delay if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms.
  • Document the breach, its effects, and the remedial actions taken.

12. Age Restriction

The Service is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected data from a person under 18, we will delete it promptly. If you believe someone under 18 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@povver.ai.
