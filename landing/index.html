Your Coach Works While You Rest

When you finish a workout, Povver's AI breaks down your session. It compares your performance to recent weeks across every muscle group, flags personal records, and spots lifts where you've been stuck at the same weight.

The app tracks progressive overload for you. Instead of digging through old logs trying to remember what you lifted last week, Povver tells you when you're ready to move up.

Ask Anything. Get a Plan in Seconds.

Tell the coach what you want in plain English. "Build me a 4-day upper/lower split" or "I need a quick leg day with dumbbells only." You get a complete program with exercises, sets, and target weights based on your training history.

During a workout, the coach switches to a faster mode. Swap an exercise because the rack is taken, check your recent numbers on a lift, or ask what weight to use next. Answers stay short because you're resting between sets, not reading essays.

A Spreadsheet You Can Take to the Gym

Every set laid out in a clean grid. Tap a cell, change the number, move on. Weight, reps, and effort level for every set, editable in any order. Need to apply the same weight across all your working sets? One tap.

Built for speed between sets. Large buttons you can hit with chalky hands, fast number entry, and nothing standing between you and your next set.

No Plan Required

Open the app and start an empty workout. Add exercises as you go, in whatever order makes sense. Or pick a saved template. Or follow your routine's next session. Or just ask the coach to build something.

Every approach works equally well. You can always add exercises, remove them, or drag to reorder mid-session. Nothing is locked down. The app fits your training style, not the other way around.
