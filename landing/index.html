Stop Wondering If You Should Add Weight

You finished your bench work. Three sets at 90, RPE crept up on the last one. Good session — but should you go heavier next week?

Povver already knows. It compares today against your recent weeks — across exercises, muscle groups, and volume — and tells you where you're ready to push, where you've stalled, and where something needs adjusting.

"Build me a 4-day upper/lower split." "Quick push day, dumbbells only." Povver generates complete programs — exercises, sets, rep ranges, target weights — all pulled from your training history. Not a template. A program that starts where you actually are.
